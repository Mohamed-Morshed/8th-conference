About

Faculty of veterinary medicine at suez canal university aims to develope scientific research skills for college students, Provide opportunity for innovation and intellectual creativity and Reducing the gap between students and faculty members, as well as creating bridges of friendship between students by unifying the goal of winning a honest competition.

The eighth student scientific conference is scheduled to be held on ninth of March, 2022.

Terms of participation

  1. The research should be prepared according to research standards.
  2. The research is under the supervision of one of the faculty members of the college.
  3. More than one student can participate in one research.
  4. The abstract should not exceed 250 words.
  5. Previous research in the college can be used.
  6. You can participate individually or with a group of your colleagues.
